designing a fidget spinner

this was the first task assigned by my teacher

it involves

1.Making a circularish(?) shape

2.Then designing a ball bearing for it

3.Making somehexagonal nuts as weights

My idea for designing it was that i wanted a fidget spinner built at an angle, so it would be more comfortable for your hand, as it dosent need to stretch to the ends of the fidget spinner

so this was the design i came up with

i made a fidget spinner, angled at a 110 degree angle around the centerline

i made it so that it wraps around the finger holding the fidget spinner

i thought it was a pretty challenging design to make

it was also fun to find out how to make the ball bearing on your own also, in my case i made mine oversized to fit around the large rim of the fidget spinner. designed to be spun around your index finger and your thumb.
